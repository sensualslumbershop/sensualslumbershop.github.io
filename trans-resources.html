Resources

Below are some useful resources that provide more information and support for transgender individuals and allies. These links lead to reputable organizations and information hubs that can offer guidance, education, and support services.

American Psychological Association - Transgender Topics

The American Psychological Association (APA) offers a comprehensive resource on transgender topics. It includes information on gender identity, the experiences of transgender individuals, and guidelines for providing supportive and effective care. The APA emphasizes the importance of understanding the challenges and discrimination faced by transgender people, as well as promoting inclusive practices.

Visit APA Transgender Topics

GLAAD LGBT+ Media News

GLAAD is an organization dedicated to promoting accurate and inclusive representation of LGBTQ+ individuals in the media. Their website offers news, resources, and information on various LGBTQ+ topics, including transgender rights and visibility. GLAAD's work helps to shape public perceptions and foster a more accepting society.

Visit GLAAD

Gender Identity Research & Education Society (GIRES)

GIRES is a UK-based organization that provides research and education on gender identity issues. They offer resources for transgender individuals, healthcare providers, and educators. GIRES works to improve the understanding and acceptance of gender diversity, promoting equality and respect for all.

Visit GIRES

Gender Identity Clinic - NHS

The Gender Identity Clinic (GIC) is part of the UK's National Health Service (NHS). It provides specialized services for individuals experiencing gender dysphoria. The clinic offers assessments, counseling, and medical treatments, including hormone therapy and surgeries. The GIC aims to support individuals in their transition process and improve their overall well-being.

Visit Gender Identity Clinic

National Center for Transgender Equality

The National Center for Transgender Equality (NCTE) is an advocacy organization in the United States. It works to advance the rights and well-being of transgender people through policy change, public education, and community engagement. NCTE provides resources on legal issues, healthcare, and social services for transgender individuals.

Visit NCTE

Stonewall UK

Stonewall is a UK-based organization that campaigns for the rights of LGBTQ+ individuals. Their work includes promoting equality in education, employment, and healthcare. Stonewall provides resources and support for transgender people, aiming to create a more inclusive and accepting society.

Visit Stonewall UK

The Trevor Project

The Trevor Project is a leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ+ youth. They offer a lifeline, chat, and text services for young people in crisis. The Trevor Project also provides educational resources and advocacy to promote mental health and well-being among LGBTQ+ youth.

Visit The Trevor Project

Transgender Law Center

The Transgender Law Center (TLC) is a national organization that advocates for the legal rights of transgender and gender nonconforming people. TLC provides legal assistance, policy advocacy, and public education to ensure that transgender individuals can live safely and authentically. Their work addresses issues such as discrimination, healthcare access, and immigration rights.

Visit Transgender Law Center

Human Rights Campaign

The Human Rights Campaign (HRC) is a major advocacy organization working to achieve equality for LGBTQ+ individuals. HRC's resources include information on transgender rights, healthcare, and workplace equality. They also provide support for families and allies of transgender people.

Visit HRC

World Professional Association for Transgender Health (WPATH)

WPATH is an international multidisciplinary professional association dedicated to promoting evidence-based care, education, research, advocacy, public policy, and respect in transgender health. They provide standards of care and resources for healthcare providers, ensuring that transgender individuals receive competent and compassionate care.

Visit WPATH

Trans Youth Equality Foundation

The Trans Youth Equality Foundation provides education, advocacy, and support for transgender and gender nonconforming youth and their families. They offer workshops, resources, and online support to help young people navigate their gender identity journey and promote acceptance and understanding in their communities.

Visit Trans Youth Equality Foundation

Trans Lifeline

Trans Lifeline is a grassroots hotline and microgrants organization offering direct emotional and financial support to transgender people in crisis. Their services are provided by and for the transgender community, helping individuals access the resources they need to survive and thrive.

Visit Trans Lifeline

Sylvia Rivera Law Project

The Sylvia Rivera Law Project (SRLP) provides free legal services to transgender, gender nonconforming, and intersex individuals who are low-income or people of color. SRLP's work includes legal advocacy, public education, and policy change to promote social, racial, and economic justice.

Visit Sylvia Rivera Law Project

Lambda Legal

Lambda Legal is a national organization committed to achieving full recognition of the civil rights of LGBTQ+ individuals and people living with HIV through impact litigation, education, and public policy work. Their resources include legal assistance, advocacy, and education on issues affecting transgender people.

Visit Lambda Legal

PFLAG

PFLAG is the first and largest organization for LGBTQ+ people, their parents and families, and allies. PFLAG provides support, education, and advocacy to promote the health and well-being of LGBTQ+ individuals and their families. They offer resources and support groups for parents and allies of transgender people.

Visit PFLAG

Gender Diversity

Gender Diversity is an organization dedicated to increasing the awareness and understanding of gender diversity in children, adolescents, and adults. They offer support groups, workshops, and resources for families, educators, and healthcare providers to create a more inclusive environment for gender-diverse individuals.

Visit Gender Diversity

Planned Parenthood - Transgender Services

Planned Parenthood offers a range of healthcare services for transgender individuals, including hormone therapy, sexual health services, and general healthcare. Their inclusive and affirming care helps transgender people access the medical support they need in a safe and welcoming environment.

Visit Planned Parenthood

International LGBTQ+ Travel Association (IGLTA)

The International LGBTQ+ Travel Association (IGLTA) provides information and resources for LGBTQ+ travelers. Their resources include travel guides, safety tips, and recommendations for LGBTQ+ friendly destinations and accommodations. IGLTA helps transgender travelers navigate their journeys with confidence and ease.

Visit IGLTA

Transgender Legal Defense & Education Fund (TLDEF)

TLDEF is committed to ending discrimination based on gender identity and expression. They provide legal representation, public education, and policy advocacy to promote equality for transgender individuals. TLDEF's work includes fighting for name change rights, healthcare access, and workplace protections for transgender people.

Visit TLDEF

ACLU - Transgender Rights

The American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) works to protect the rights and liberties of all people, including transgender individuals. Their transgender rights project focuses on legal advocacy, public education, and policy change to ensure that transgender people can live freely and without discrimination.

Visit ACLU Transgender Rights

Outsports - Transgender Athletes

Outsports is a leading voice in LGBTQ+ sports, providing news, analysis, and resources for transgender athletes. They promote visibility and inclusion in sports, highlighting the achievements and challenges of transgender athletes at all levels of competition.

Visit Outsports

Revel & Riot - Trans Resources

Revel & Riot is an organization that promotes LGBTQ+ rights and awareness through art, design, and resources. Their trans resources page offers a wide range of information, including healthcare, legal rights, and community support for transgender individuals and their allies.

Visit Revel & Riot
